1. <span data-ttu-id="ab3f1-106">Product information management > Products > Released products (Ürün bilgi yönetimi > Ürünler > Piyasaya sürülmüş ürünler) menüsüne gidin.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ab3f1-106">Go to Product information management > Products > Released products.</span></span>
