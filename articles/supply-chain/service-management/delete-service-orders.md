[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="0fc8c-104">**Servis yönetimi** \> **Periyodik** \> **Servis siparişleri** \> **Servis siparişlerini sil**'e tıklayın.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fc8c-104">Click **Service management** \> **Periodic** \> **Service orders** \> **Delete service orders**.</span></span>
