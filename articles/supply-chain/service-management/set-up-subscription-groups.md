[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="92be5-104">**Hizmet yönetimi** \> **Kurulum** \> **Servis abonelikleri** \> **Abonelik grupları**'na gidin.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="92be5-104">Go to **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **Subscription groups**.</span></span>
