[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="5413c-104">**Servis yönetimi** \> **Ortak** \> **Servis sözleşmeleri** \> **Servis sözleşmeleri**'ne tıklayın.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5413c-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5413c-105">Servis ürün reçetesini silmek istediğiniz servis sözleşmesini seçin.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5413c-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>
